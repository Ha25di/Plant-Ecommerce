Who Are We?

At Zaatar, we unite Lebanon's ancient love for nature with modern convenience. Founded in 2023, our family-run venture aims to nurture a greener future by making plant care and gardening accessible to everyone. From the Cedars to the Bekaa Valley, our country's rich landscapes inspire our curated selection of plants and the knowledge we share.

We believe that every plant we bring into our homes enriches our lives, serving as a testament to the beauty and versatility of Lebanese flora.
